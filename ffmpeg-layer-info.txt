FFmpeg Lambda Layer (deployed from AWS Serverless Application Repository)

Steps (Serverless Application Repository):
1. Visit the SAR app (select ffmpeg-lambda-layer).
2. Click Deploy and follow prompts (region, application settings).
3. After deployment finishes, open the AWS Lambda > Layers console (or the SAR deployment output). Copy the Layer ARN or the Layer's physical ID (ARN looks like: arn:aws:lambda:<region>:<account-id>:layer:<layer-name>:<version>).
4. In your Lambda function configuration, under Layers -> Specify an ARN, paste the Layer ARN and add the layer to your function.

Notes:
- After adding the layer, the FFmpeg binary is typically available at /opt/bin/ffmpeg in the Lambda runtime.

Example placeholder (replace after your deployment):
arn:aws:lambda:<region>:<account-id>:layer:ffmpeg-layer-from-sar:1
